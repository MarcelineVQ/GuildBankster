Guild Bankster
===

An addon for Turtle WoW that provides template-based guild bank management and automated restocking.

## Features

- **Template System**: Create templates for your guild bank tabs to define what items should be stored where
- **Automated Restocking**: Automatically restock guild bank slots based on your templates using items from your inventory and personal bank
- **Visual Integration**: Seamlessly integrates with the Turtle WoW guild bank UI

## Usage

### Setting up Templates

1. **Access Template Mode**: Open the guild bank and click the "Templates" tab at the bottom
2. **Select Template Tab**: Click on tabs to switch between different template configurations
3. **Create Template**:
   - Drag items from your inventory onto template slots to set what should be stored there
   - Right-click on template slots to clear them
   - Right-click with an item on cursor to clear the cursor

### Managing Templates

- **Activate/Deactivate**: Right-click on template tabs to toggle them between active/inactive
  - Active templates: Will be included in restocking operations
  - Inactive templates (default): Will be ignored during restocking
- **Switch Templates**: Left-click on template tabs to view and edit different templates

### Restocking

1. **Prepare**: Make sure you have the items you want to deposit in your inventory and/or personal bank
2. **Open Guild Bank**: Access your guild bank
3. **Start Restock**: Click the "Restock" button (located at bottom-right of guild bank frame)
4. **Automatic Process**: The addon will:
   - Only process templates marked as "active"
   - Switch to each tab automatically so you can observe progress
   - Deposit items from your inventory/bank to match template quantities

## Bank NPCs for Enhanced Banking

Restocking the Guild Bank from your Player Bank can be done by having a Player Bank open via a mobile bank when using the Guild Bank interface, but characters without a mobile bank can still bank restock by speaking with the following npcs:

* **Horde banks:**
  * Orgrimmar: Koma
  * Undercity: Randolph Montague
  * Thunder Bluff: Chesmu
* **Alliance banks:**
  * Darnassus: Any teller
